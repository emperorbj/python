5. General Policies
5.1. Data Privacy
InnovateX Inc. is committed to protecting the privacy of personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws. Our Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information.

5.2. Intellectual Property
All intellectual property developed by employees in the course of their employment with InnovateX Inc. belongs to the company. This includes, but is not limited to, software, inventions, designs, and creative works.

5.3. Social Media Guidelines
Employees representing InnovateX Inc. on social media platforms must do so responsibly and professionally, adhering to our brand guidelines and respecting confidentiality. When using personal social media, employees should exercise good judgment and avoid discussing confidential company information or making disparaging remarks about the company, its employees, or its customers.

5.4. Whistleblower Policy
InnovateX Inc. encourages employees to report any unethical or illegal conduct without fear of retaliation. Our Whistleblower Policy outlines the process for reporting concerns and protections for whistleblowers.
4. Employee Policies
4.1. Equal Employment Opportunity
InnovateX Inc. is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees, regardless of race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or veteran status.

4.2. Workplace Conduct
All employees are expected to conduct themselves professionally and respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or any form of disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

4.3. Confidentiality and Data Security
Employees must protect InnovateX Inc.'s confidential information, including proprietary technology, customer data, and business strategies. Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information is strictly prohibited. All company data must be handled in accordance with our data security protocols.

4.4. Code of Ethics
Employees are expected to adhere to the InnovateX Inc. Code of Ethics, which outlines our commitment to honesty, fairness, and ethical conduct in all business dealings. A full copy of the Code of Ethics is available on the company intranet.

4.5. Internet and Email Usage
Company-provided internet and email resources are primarily for business purposes. Incidental personal use is permitted but must not interfere with work duties or violate any company policies. Accessing or distributing inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.

4.6. Workplace Safety
InnovateX Inc. is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. Employees are responsible for adhering to all safety guidelines and reporting any hazards or incidents immediately.

4.7. Anti-Bribery and Corruption
InnovateX Inc. has a zero-tolerance policy for bribery and corruption. Employees must not offer, give, solicit, or accept any bribes or improper payments.
